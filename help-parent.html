RackStat Parent Guide

Parent Portal Overview

This guide explains what parents and guardians can view inside RackStat and how read-only access works.

1. Parent Access

Parent access is approved by coaching staff. Once approved, parents can view linked athlete information in a read-only format.

2. Linked Athletes

The Parent Portal displays athletes that have been approved and linked to your account. If no athlete appears, access may still be pending or not yet approved.

3. What You Can View

  • Current strength metrics
  • Bodyweight and performance indicators
  • Recent workout activity
  • Recent nutrition logs, if used by the athlete
  • Verified personal records approved by coaching staff
  • Weekly activity summaries

4. Read-Only Access

Parent accounts cannot edit athlete data, submit lifts, change meals, approve records, or adjust team settings.

5. If Something Looks Missing

Some information only appears after the athlete logs activity or after a coach approves submitted performance data.

  • No workouts shown may mean the athlete has not logged recent sessions.
  • No PRs shown may mean no records have been approved yet.
  • No nutrition logs shown may mean the athlete is not using meal tracking.

Important Note

RackStat is a performance tracking tool. It does not provide medical, nutrition, or training advice.
