Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

RACKSTAT is designed to support student-athletes and coaching staff in tracking athletic development, training performance, and nutrition habits in a structured, team-controlled environment.

Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following information:

  • Student name and school affiliation
  • Strength and performance metrics (e.g., lifts, bodyweight, scores)
  • Nutritional and meal tracking data
  • Basic profile information provided by the athlete or coach

How Data Is Used

This data is used strictly for athletic development, team performance tracking, and communication between athletes and authorized coaching staff.

Data Access & Control

  • Access is limited to the student-athlete and authorized coaches within the same program
  • Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties
  • No data is used for advertising or marketing purposes

Parental Rights

For student-athletes under 18, parents or legal guardians have the right to request access to their child’s data at any time. Requests should be made through the athlete’s coaching staff or program administrator.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect stored data from unauthorized access. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as the athlete is part of the program, unless a deletion request is made by the athlete, parent/guardian, or coaching staff.

Disclaimer: RACKSTAT is not a medical or nutritional provider. Information within the platform should not be considered medical advice.
